The Studio


We are located in the Grant Park neighborhood of Atlanta, less than a mile from the Atlanta Zoo and on the soon open souther section of the Beltline.


We have air conditioning and lots of windows. Parking is right next to the studio. We're in the Artists Cove section of the Beacon complex. There are restaurants, shops and bars in the complex so come and spend the whole day here.
